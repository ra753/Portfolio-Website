How to Setup a Blog For Free?

I always wanted to write something on things I studied or things which fascinated me. Writing something which no one can read is quite discouraging and fruitless hence, maintaining a blog and writing something on it regularly can titillate and amplify your thoughts.

So, Where to Start?

1. Write a site - Preferably use Bootstrap as it is easiest to catch on and results are noteworthy. Don’t Worry if doing from scratch is not your way then there are thousands of templates available for free, just search for them. For making a more efficient website in terms of content management, you can use Jekyll, it is a static site generator which is specifically made for creating blogs and it can be easily integrated with various Content Management Systems (CMS). While learning curve of such frameworks can be steep, but once you overcome, everything becomes breezy.

2. Upload it on GitHub – This is where the magic happens, usually hosting can cost you a lot, but the Spartan GitHub is providing it for free. Follow this article to see this in detail.

3. Purchase a Domain – Well, we have got a bottleneck for buying a domain you may have to shell out few hundred rupees every year but to avoid it we are going to use Freenom, it provides free domains for a year (God Bless them!).

4. Finishing Up – Now we have got everything to go live on WWW, follow this video it will help you to integrate newly acquired domain along with GitHub hosting.

5. Profit

Now, write your heart out on your own brand new blog.
